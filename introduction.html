Introduction to Cyber Literacy

What is Cyber Literacy?

In today's world, the internet is a big part of our daily lives. Whether it's talking to family, shopping online, or managing our finances, we all use the internet for many things. But with all these benefits comes some risks. There are people out there who try to trick us into giving away our personal information, which can lead to problems like identity theft or financial loss.

Cybercrime, which includes these tricky scams, has been growing rapidly. It's important for everyone, especially those who might not be as familiar with technology, to learn some basic online safety skills. These skills can help you protect your information and feel more secure when using the internet.

This tutorial is designed specifically for you, with simple and easy-to-understand explanations. We'll talk about how to recognize and avoid common online scams, and how to stay safe while enjoying all the benefits the internet has to offer.

Remember, it's never too late to learn these skills, and taking a little time to understand them can save you from a lot of trouble in the future. Let's learn how to stay safe online together and make the most of the digital world!
