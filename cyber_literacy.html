Session 1: Understanding Cyber Literacy

What is Cyber Literacy?

Cyber literacy is about knowing how to use the internet safely and wisely. It helps you protect your personal information, like your bank details or Social Security number, from scammers.

Why It's Important:

  • Protects Your Privacy: Keeps your personal information safe.
  • Prevents Identity Theft: Stops others from pretending to be you online.
  • Secures Your Finances: Guards your money from online thieves.

Example:

  • Imagine getting an email saying you've won a prize but need to share your bank details. Cyber literacy helps you recognize this as a scam.

    • Key Benefits:

    • Avoid online scams.
    • Feel confident using technology.
    • Enjoy the internet without worry.

    Quick Tip: Always double-check emails or messages asking for personal information. If you didn't start the conversation, be cautious.
